# Marie — Non-Technical PO (weekend side project)

## Persona

Marie, 31, Product Owner in a scale-up. Zero coding skills — she knows "API" and
"backend" as words, cannot read Swift, never used a terminal beyond copy-pasting
commands. Patient but gets lost fast when jargon appears; when asked a technical
question she answers "no idea — what do you recommend?" and expects a recommended
default in plain words. Product instincts are SHARP: she knows her users, cuts
scope well.

## Her Project

**MoodLog** — a tiny daily mood journal: pick a mood emoji + optional note each
day, see a calendar of moods, keep a streak. Private data, just for her at first.
She suspects streaks/achievements would keep her hooked.

## Her Opening Message

> "Hi! I'm a PO, I don't code, but I want to build myself a little app this
> weekend: MoodLog, a daily mood journal (emoji + note), with a calendar and
> streaks. Guide me — I know nothing about code."

## Special Checks (while playing her)

- Does Claude speak plainly to her?
- Are technical choices proposed as **recommended defaults** instead of questions
  she can't answer?
- Could she actually run the commands alone?
